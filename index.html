Performance Overview

Key Insights

Strategic Leavers initiatives have delivered outstanding performance through June 2025, with exceptional results across both Strategic Levers and UAE initiatives. Strategic Levers achieved remarkable YTD performance with 161.7% impressions achievement (10.54B vs 6.52B forecast), 139.2% engagement achievement (1.44B vs 1.03B forecast), and outstanding 174.1% paid sessions achievement (12.53M vs 7.20M forecast). Strategic Levers UAE also delivered strong results with 133.4% impressions achievement (1.72B vs 1.29B forecast), 107.1% engagement achievement (159.5M vs 149.0M forecast), and excellent 169.8% paid sessions achievement (1.36M vs 802K forecast). June was particularly strong with Strategic Levers achieving 175.1% impressions performance and Strategic Levers UAE delivering 103.1% impressions achievement, maintaining robust momentum through the first half of 2025.
